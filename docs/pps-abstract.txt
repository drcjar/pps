Study Summary

Title: Pleural Plaque Study (PPS).

Design: Ecological study.

Aim: To characterize and measure changes in the distribution of pleural
plaques in
the population over time.

Outcome measures: 1. Geospatial K-means clustering of pleural plaques by
5 year
epoch. 2. Correlation with pleural mesothelioma mortality data.

Population: Patients with a CT scan that includes their pleura.

Eligibility: Meets population definition, able to give informed consent.

Duration: One year.
